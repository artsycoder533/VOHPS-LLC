Our Mission

We are pleased that you selected Visions of Hope Psychological Services, LLC (VOHPS) for your assistance. With over 14 years of professional experience, our hallmark has been focused on attention to individual needs, competently delivered with compassion and sensitivity. Our belief is that we all possess the tools, resources, and capacities to heal, and we pride ourselves on helping to facilitate restoration, growth, and change. Our mission is to:

  • Provide a supportive, nurturing environment for individuals.
  • Facilitate positive family, peer, and community relationships.
  • Empower individuals to reach their full potential and provide them with the tools needed to succeed in society.
  • Strive to teach and instill in clients a sense of strength and resilience by providing education to assist them in adequately identifying and addressing issues as they arise.
  • Provide an atmosphere for healing, growth, perspective, and insight.
  • Challenge clients to be the best version of themselves by incorporating a variety of therapeutic techniques and evidence-based practices.
  • Create a safe space that will allow individuals to find strength and security in their vulnerability.

Dr. Selisha Nelson PhD, LCP

I am a Licensed Clinical Psychologist with a passion for helping others on their journeys to healing, self-discovery, and growth. I have experience working with youth, adolescents and adults providing individual, family, and couples therapy. Psychological assessments are also provided. I have obtained advanced training in trauma treatment, which includes various modalities such as art/play therapy, Somatic Experiencing, and EMDR. I have over 14 years of experience working in community mental health, alternative school settings, residential treatment, outpatient settings, and correctional facilities.

I have clinical experience in the following modalities: Trauma treatment, Somatic Experiencing, Cognitive-behavioral therapy, Art/Play therapy, Dialectical Behavioral therapy (DBT), Attachment, Couples/Marriage therapy, Eye Movement Desensitization Reprocessing (EMDR), and Intensive evidence-based treatments for a myriad of challenging mental health disorders.

I graduated in 2016 from Walden University with a Doctorate in Clinical Psychology. I have a warm and gentle, yet, no nonsense approach to therapy. I am passionate about helping others and incorporate a fun, experiential, and creative approach to therapy

Frequently Asked Questions

Why choose counseling?


Counseling affords benefits to the individual, including the prospect of reducing or eliminating distressing psychological symptoms, enhancing interpersonal relationships and resolving life crises or concerns. To be successful, counseling presumes that you are honest, open and active in expressing your thoughts, behaviors, and emotions. Often counseling may evoke strong feelings, which may include worries, fears, sadness, anxiety or depression. Expressing such feelings is a basic element of counseling and encouraged. Acting out, however, in violent or destructive ways is not condoned and may result in termination of services and/or communication of such patterns to other health care providers or law enforcement officials as a means of addressing safety.
