About

"Our belief is that we all possess the tools, resources, and capacities to heal. We pride ourselves on helping to facilitate resoration, growth, and change"

-Dr. Selisha Nelson PhD, LCP

Who we are:

  • A mental health private practice
  • 14 years of professional counseling experience.
  • Owned and operated by Dr. Selisha Nelson PhD, LCP.
  • Accredited by the Virginia Board of Psychology

What we do:

  • Facilitate positive peer, family, and community relationships.
  • Empower individuals to reach their full potential and provide them with the tools needed to succeed in society.
  • Teach and instill in clients a sense of strength and resilience.
  • Provide education to assist clients to adequately identify and address issues as they arise

How we do it:

  • Challenge clients to be the best version of themselves
  • Incorporate a variety of therapeautic techniques and evidence-based practices
  • create a safe space that will allow individuals to find strength and security in their vulnerability.
  • American Psychological Association Member
  • Provide an aptmosphere for healing, growth, perspective, and insight.
